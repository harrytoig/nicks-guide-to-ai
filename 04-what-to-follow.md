# What to Follow

*Podcasts, blogs, and X accounts worth your time.*

Pick 2 podcasts, 2 newsletters, and a dozen X follows. Consistency beats breadth. If you try to follow everyone, you follow no one. The goal is calibrated intuition, not winning a Jeopardy category.

---

## Podcasts (what's actually in my Spotify)

### AI-focused
- **AI Daily Brief** (Nathaniel Whittemore): 10 minutes a day. Best signal-to-noise for AI news. My morning default.
- **TBPN**: tech, markets, AI. Go for the shortened versions. The full episodes run long.
- **No Priors** (Sarah Guo + Elad Gil): AI-focused, VC-flavored. Guests actually run the industry.
- **Training Data** (Sequoia): short, founder-focused AI interviews.
- **Latent Space** (swyx + Alessio): the builder's show. Infra, evals, agent frameworks.
- **How I AI** (Claire Vo): how people actually use AI in their work, week by week.

### Broader tech rotation
- **Stratechery** (Ben Thompson): strategy takes on tech and AI. Also the paid newsletter below.
- **All-In**: Chamath, Jason, Sacks, Friedberg. Skip heavy politics; the tech/markets takes are the reason.
- **Lenny's Podcast**: PM + career. The Boris Cherny episode on Claude Code is a must.
- **BG2Pod** (Brad Gerstner + Bill Gurley): tech investing, strategic.
- **Y Combinator Startup Podcast**: founder-focused.
- **This Week in Startups** (Jason Calacanis).
- **Moonshots** (Peter Diamandis): future tech, optimism-heavy.
- **My First Million**: entrepreneurship.

### Worth adding for depth
- **[Dwarkesh Podcast](https://www.dwarkesh.com/podcast)**: long, unhurried interviews with the people actually building frontier AI. Dario Amodei, Demis Hassabis, Ilya Sutskever, Karpathy, Zuck. Widely considered the single best AI podcast. I don't currently listen, but you should.

## Reading

### What I pay for
- **[Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/)** (Ben Thompson, paid): clearest strategic thinking on what AI means for incumbents, platforms, and power. Worth every dollar.

### Worth adding
- **[Simon Willison's weblog](https://simonwillison.net/)** (free): the single best daily AI writer working. Ships tools, tests every model, links everything that matters. If he posts it, read it.
- **[Import AI](https://importai.substack.com/)** (Jack Clark, Anthropic cofounder, free): weekly. Policy, capability jumps, strategic framing from inside the frontier.

## How I Actually Use This

I do not keep up with everything. Here is the real rhythm:

- **Morning (10 min):** AI Daily Brief on the subway.
- **Commute / walks:** TBPN shortened versions. All-In or BG2Pod when it's a long flight.
- **Weekly:** Stratechery when Ben ships a big one.
- **Ambient:** X feed, tightly curated to the accounts above. Mute generously when it turns into noise.

Consistency beats volume. If you're consuming more than you're building, your ratio is off. Close the tab and go make something.
